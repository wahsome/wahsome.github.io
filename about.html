About Me




Cindy Wah

I am currently attending The Art Institute striving to obtain a Bachelors Degree in Graphic/Web Design. Since I was a little kid, I have always liked the arts and enjoyed painting, coloring, and drawing. However, after high school I took a detour and joined the United States Air Force. It was an honor to have served this amazing country. After 10 years and a tour to Afganistan I deciced to follow my true passion in the arts.

I love every moment I spend working on new projects and learning new techniques. I will obtain my degree in Graphic and Web Design in 2018. Please follow me on Instagram for my most recent designs. Thank you for taking the time to visit my Website.
